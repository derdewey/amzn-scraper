Amazon Verified Purchase(What's this?)
This review is from: Cole Haan Hand-Stained Leather Kindle Cover with Hinge (Fits 6" Display, Latest Generation Kindle), Green Floral Print (Accessory)
A lovely cover that feels great to hold but I am returning mine. I need more protection than this cover offers. As a previous reviewer noted the only thing holding your Kindle in place is the hinge. I would have kept this cover if there had been anyway to secure the cover while in my bag. This would be a great cover for those that don't take their Kindle out of the house with them very often and/or need a soft, fairly light and pliable cover.

Pros-
It's a Cole Haan, the leather and tea stained design are beautiful.
Very soft in the hand and folds back very easily.
Accessory pocket.

Cons-
It flops open easily with no way to keep it closed. This was the deal breaker for me.

I hated the Amazon covers for my K1 and K2 but I really like the K3 cover with the light. I'll wait to see what else comes from other manufacturers because I love to have a variety of covers.

3.0 out of 5 stars Quality Leather, nice cover, September 8, 2010
By 
S. Levin "Chick with Books" (Danbury, CT USA) - See all my reviews
(REAL NAME)   
Amazon Verified Purchase(What's this?)
This review is from: Cole Haan Hand-Stained Leather Kindle Cover with Hinge (Fits 6" Display, Latest Generation Kindle), Green Floral Print (Accessory)
Loving my previous Cole Haan Leather Kindle Cover for my Kindle 2, I was happy to see that Cole Haan came out with the covers for the Kindle 3. The leather isn't as soft and supple on this leather cover, possibly due to the fact it is hand-stained and imprinted, but has a nice feel none the less... Also, I can easily open & flip the cover over to rest on the back so I can hold with one hand.

* The color is deceptive in the photo Amazon provides. This is a light sea foam green. Very pretty, especially with my white Kindle 3, but the photo makes this cover to appear almost white,and it definitely is NOT that light.

* I only have one negative, and that would be the hinge area. There is no reinforcement inside where the clips protrude from the leather. In the previous Cole Haan covers there was a leather piece that reinforced this area and helped to prevent possible wear or rips to this point in the leather.

All in all, a nice product from Cole Haan!
